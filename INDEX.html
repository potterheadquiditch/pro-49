lantern

step-1 ->take a square paper and fold it half to form a rectangle.open the fold and fold it from another side to form a rectangle

step-2 ->open the fold you will get two half lines. now take a corner of the paper bring its tip to the middle point

step-3 ->do this for all corners

step-4 ->flip the paper and agian fold the corners

step-5 ->now you will get four four corners with a intersection between them open one of those intersection and the one opposite of it

now your lantern is ready!
